About Us

Al-Thaqlain International Recruitment Company assists companies with entry and operations in the Iraq and Afghanistan global marketplaces. Since 2008, our experts have been providing knowledge and transparency to companies worldwide, helping them approach their business and project development within Iraq and Afghanistan.
