About Us

Al-Thaqlain International Recruitment Company assists companies with entry and operations in the Iraq and Afghanistan global marketplaces. Since 2008, our experts have been providing knowledge and transparency to companies worldwide, helping them approach their business and project development within Iraq and Afghanistan. We understand that these markets have complex hurdles to overcome, and the experts at Al-Thaqlain International Recruitment Company possess the business relationships and industry know-how to navigate your company through this uncharted territory.

The focus of Al-Thaqlain International Recruitment Company is to provide a full range of consultancy and business solutions to investors and corporations interested in the Iraq and Afghanistan markets or already operating there. We offer our clients services including Visa Facilitation, Manpower Supply, Life Support (Catering & Mineral Water Supply), and Heavy Machinery Supply.
