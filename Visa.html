Visa Solution to Afghanistan

Single Entry Tourist visas

Single-entry tourist visas allow travelers to enter a country once for leisure or sightseeing within a specified period, typically ranging from a few weeks to several months. After exiting, re-entry is not permitted on the same visa, requiring a new application for subsequent visits.

UMulti Entry Visas.

Multi-entry visas permit travelers to enter and exit a country multiple times within the visa's validity period, offering flexibility for frequent visits. This type of visa is ideal for business travelers or individuals with personal commitments requiring repeated travel.

BMOFA facilitation

MOFA facilitation streamlines the visa application process through the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, ensuring efficient handling of diplomatic and official travel requests. This service often includes assistance with documentation, expedited processing, and support for complex visa requirements.

Visa Solution to Iraq

Single and Multi-Entry Visas

Single-entry visas allow travelers to enter a country only once within a specified period, while multi-entry visas permit multiple entries during their validity, facilitating frequent travel for business or personal reasons.

Urgent Visas & Paper Visas

Urgent visas expedite the application process for travelers needing immediate travel authorization, often within 24 to 72 hours. Paper visas, on the other hand, require physical documentation and in-person submissions, contrasting with the convenience of electronic visas.

Blood Test & Residency Visas

Blood tests are often required for residency visas to screen for certain diseases, ensuring public health safety. Residency visas permit long-term or permanent stay in a foreign country, often necessitating additional health and security checks.

Airport Meet and Greet

Airport meet and greet services provide personalized assistance upon arrival, including expedited immigration processing and baggage handling, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free entry experience.
